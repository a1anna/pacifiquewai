The story behind the sip

Born from island roots. Made for your moment of rest.

Pacifique Wai was created to feel like the first deep breath after a long season of nonstop work.

Our drinks are inspired by island flavors, family gatherings, warm sunsets, cool trade winds, and the kind of moments that remind us to slow down and enjoy where we are.

Every cup is crafted to bring a little more color, joy, and vacation energy into your day—whether you find us at a market, a celebration, or somewhere along your island journey.
